Namrata Ecocity

If your campaign is born out of an exciting idea and matches with the brand promise; it really creates wonders. Our ‘Parivartan’ (which means change) campaign for ‘Ecocity’- a residential project by Namrata Group, just proved that. Since ‘Ecocity’ was launched in Talegaon, the campaign talked about the refreshing change it would breathe in the life of an Ecocity dweller. The model photo shoot brought the whole endeavour to life!
