Water's Squares

The challenge of ‘multiple’ transcended by one solution

Project Water’s Square by sagar properties was looking for an expression after its arrival in life. A fusion of – office space, hypermarket, shopping and restaurant arena in a single premise asked for an eloquent route and efficiently smart advertising. As the entity bloomed with multiple facets, we came up with one solution giving ample recognition to each one. A 4-pager was designed and released in the newspapers complimenting all the elements of the project – Water’s Square. Outdoor advertising through hoardings also helped highlighting the project upfront!
