TMR Group

Task

TMR Group, one of the leading plotting project developers in Hyderabad, needed to make its brand presence felt strongly with hard-hitting campaigns across the cyber city.

  • Corporate Branding
  • Environmental branding
  • Digital Media Promotions
  • Corporate AV
  • Promotional Campaigns
Solution

Having an innate understanding of the aesthetics and communication that strike an emotional chord with the audiences, 3 Dots Design helped elevate the brand visibility and image of TMR Group - a premier plotting project developer in Hyderabad. TMR Group has a rich legacy of land development in Hyderabad, with 16 years of core expertise in plotting projects. Helping the brand re-emphasize its position in the market scenario, 3 Dots launched a clutter-breaking corporate ad in The Times of India, Hyderabad. Additionally, 3 Dots Design is also serving as the digital marketing agency, Hyderabad for catering to TMR Group’s online branding needs.

  • ClientTMR Group, Hyderabad
  • IndustryPlots Developer
  • ServicesBRAND LAUNCH & PROMOTIONS, STRATEGY, PRINT MEDIA ADVERTISING, DIGITAL MARKETING, EVENTS, WEBSITE
TMR Group is on a marketing drive and 3 Dots Design is making it happen seamlessly.

With all these exciting new events going on with TMR Group, it was time for a completely distinct brand identity to make its mark. Thus, taking the elements of the logo and utilising them creatively, we created the entire corporate kit. From Stationary and Visiting Cards to corporate giveaways like branded mugs, 3 Dots Design made sure that the identity of TMR Group is etched firmly in the minds of the consumer. The entire idea was to make TMR Group synonymous with plots so much so that when they ‘think plots, think TMR Group’!
